About Me

A news anchor and reporter with 5+ years of on- Air new experience, competent with technicalities of electronic media like Octopus software and Hindi typing proficiency.
I am a person with big ambitions. From the time I passed out my school, I started working in the media field. It eventually grew up from a news reader of a portal to a News presenter of a reputed channel. I achieved many awards in my school and college. I love new opportunities that knock on my door.
Endowed with good listening and debating skills, I am a very spontaneous and punctual person
who can efficiently multi-task and think well on my feet
